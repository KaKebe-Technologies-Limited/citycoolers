Welcome to City Coolers Engineering Ltd

Your trusted partner for commercial and industrial refrigeration solutions

City Coolers Engineering Limited is a Ugandan based incorporated company situated in Kampala City. City Coolers Engineering Ltd offers sales and services such as: Air Conditioning & Cold room/freezer, Mechanical Works, Electrical, Security & Alarm Systems.

The demand & requirements for electrical, air conditioning, coldroom/freezer, mechanical works, security & alarm systems has taken a new trend due to progressive increase in population, offices, homes & industrial consumption level which has tremendously increased. This comes with a challenge of sourcing for well qualified/certified engineers & technical team to install & service the equipments for maximum benefit in the fast and competitive world.

City Coolers Engineering Limited has an aggregate experience with certified and highly skilled engineers and technical teams that offer to our client's satisfactory results and long lasting solutions to their concerns/problems. Not only do our certified technical team specialize in the latest technologies and delivery of unmatched results, but we are also dedicated to learning and supporting your business as a whole by providing you with a cost efficient solutions.

We deliver unprecedented business for our clients through a commitment to qualify, safety & protection of environment, efficient utilization of capital assets and higher standard risk management.
