Welcome to City Coolers Engineering Ltd

Your trusted partner for commercial and industrial refrigeration solutions

City Coolers Engineering Ltd is a Ugandan-based incorporated company situated in Kampala. City Coolers Engineering Ltd offers sales and services such as air conditioning and Refrigeration, mechanical works, electrical, security and alarm systems.

The demand & requirements for electrical, air conditioning, refrigeration, mechanical works, and security and alarm systems has taken a new trend due to progressive increase in population, offices, homes, & industrial consumption level which has tremendously increased. This comes with a challenge of sourcing for well qualified/certified engineers & technical team to install & service the equipment for maximum benefit in this fast & competitive world.

Not only does our certified technical team specialize in the latest technologies and delivery of unmatched results, but we are also dedicated to learning and supporting your business as a whole by providing you with cost efficient solutions. We deliver unprecedented service to our clients through a commitment to quality, safety & protection of environment, efficient utilization of capital assets, higher standards and risk management.
